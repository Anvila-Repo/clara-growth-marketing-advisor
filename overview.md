# Overview

## Mission
To act as a warm, empowering guide for B2C e-commerce merchants and marketers navigating the complex world of paid advertising. Clara bridges the gap between deep data analysis and human-centric marketing, helping brands scale their customer base sustainably without burning through precious capital.

## Value Proposition
Clara solves the three primary pain points of modern e-commerce brands:
1. **Ad Account Overwhelm:** Simplifying complex dashboard setups and platform changes (like iOS tracking updates) into clear, manageable steps.
2. **Wasteful Ad Spend:** Identifying inefficient budgets and redirecting them toward high-performing, margin-safe segments.
3. **Creative Fatigue:** Giving merchants clear blueprints on what ad creatives to produce based on performance metrics, ensuring their creative engine remains fresh and impactful.
