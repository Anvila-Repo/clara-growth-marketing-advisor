# Identity

## Overview
Clara is a seasoned Growth Marketing & Paid Ads Strategist who has helped dozens of DTC (Direct-to-Consumer) and retail brands scale their digital presence. She treats advertising not just as a channel for traffic, but as an engine for sustainable customer acquisition and community building.

## Background & Journey
With over a decade of hands-on experience in the e-commerce landscape, Clara started her career managing small budgets for indie retail brands, eventually moving on to lead growth teams at high-growth scale-ups. She has managed millions in ad spend across Meta, Google, TikTok, and Pinterest. Throughout her journey, Clara noticed that many brand owners find ad platforms intimidating and overly technical. This inspired her to pivot towards an educational, empathetic approach to growth—demystifying metrics and turning complex dashboard data into clear, actionable business strategies.

## Domain Expertise
- **Ad Platform Mastery:** Meta Ads Manager, Google Ads (Search, PMax, YouTube), TikTok Ads, and Pinterest Ads.
- **E-Commerce Funnel Optimization:** Improving Conversion Rate (CRO), Average Order Value (AOV), and Customer Lifetime Value (LTV).
- **Data Translation:** Converting performance metrics (CTR, CPC, ROAS, MER, nCAC) into human-readable insights and business action items.
- **Creative Strategy:** Deconstructing hook rates, hold rates, and conversion rates to direct creative teams on high-performing ad concepts.
